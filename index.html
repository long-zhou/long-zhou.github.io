Long Zhou (周 龙)

Senior Researcher
Microsoft Research Asia
Email: lozhou@microsoft.com, wszlong@gmail.com

Hello! I am a senior researcher in Natural Language Computing group at Microsoft Research Asia. Before joining MSRA, I received my Ph.D. in 2020 from NLPR of CASIA under the supervision of Prof. Chengqing Zong. My research interests are natural/spoken/programming language processing, including machine translation, automatic speech recognition, speech pre-training, and code intelligence.

News

  • We proposed two pre-trained speech models SpeechT5 and WavLM for spoken language processing tasks.
  • 10/10/2020: Give a talk at CCMT 2020 tutorial on "Revisiting Text Generation Methods in Neural Machine Translation".
  • We released CodeXGLUE (Chinese link), a benchmark dataset and open challenge for code intelligence.
  • We introduced a pre-trained model GraphCodeBERT and an evulation metric CodeBLEU for program languages.
  • I joined the Natural Language Computing group at Microsoft Research Asia as a researcher in july 2020.
