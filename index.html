Hello! I am a researcher in Natural Language Computing group at Microsoft Research Asia. Before joining MSRA, I received my Ph.D. in 2020 from NLPR of CASIA under the supervision of Prof. Chengqing Zong. My research interests include natural language processing, machine translation, and code intelligence.

News

  • 10/10/2020: Give a talk at CCMT 2020 tutorial on "Revisiting Text Generation Methods in Neural Machine Translation".
  • We released CodeXGLUE (Chinese link), a benchmark dataset and open challenge for code intelligence.
  • We introduced a pre-trained model GraphCodeBERT and an evulation metric CodeBLEU for program languages.
  • I joined the Natural Language Computing group at Microsoft Research Asia as a researcher in july 2020.
